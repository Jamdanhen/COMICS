## Core Idea

The first comic dictionary page should introduce a small number of high-weight symbols rather than the full resident list.

The current best shortlist:

- Around Here
- The Noodle
- The Rubber Chicken
- The Rook
- The Crate
- Zodak's Helmet

## Why These Six

These symbols carry the most conceptual weight while still being readable without too much explanation.

They define the jurisdiction, the unresolved thing, the absurdity check, the structure marker, classified unknowns, and unresolved classification.

## Page Function

This page should not feel like a complete encyclopedia.

It should feel like the first dictionary page somebody finds after realizing they are already Around Here.

## Around Here Sign Note

Around Here needs a sign.

The sign is important because every mythology eventually needs a `You Are Here` marker.

The joke:

The sign does not help anyone find their position.

It only confirms that they have already arrived.
