## Core Visual Idea

Around Here should be represented by an ordinary sign that behaves like a mythological `You Are Here` marker.

The sign does not help the reader locate themselves geographically.

It only confirms that they have already arrived within the jurisdiction.

## Best Current Form

A weathered, hand-painted roadside sign on a simple post.

Text:

```text
YOU ARE
AROUND HERE
```

The sign should feel:

- ordinary at first glance
- slightly handmade
- a little wrong
- readable from a distance
- funny because it is unhelpful
- mythic because it names the jurisdiction

## Directional Joke

The arrow should not provide useful navigation.

Possible arrow treatments:

- crooked arrow
- arrow pointing off the sign
- arrow bending around itself
- arrow pointing both ways
- arrow pointing at the words instead of a direction
- arrow pointing toward a nearby object that is clearly not a destination

The point is not confusion for confusion's sake.

The point is:

> You are here, but that fact does not tell you where here is.

## Symbolic Function

The sign represents:

- jurisdiction
- provenance
- arrival
- the place where observations become mythology
- permission for unresolved things to remain unresolved

It should not represent:

- a literal town
- a country
- a city limit
- a map marker
- a fantasy portal
- a psychological state
