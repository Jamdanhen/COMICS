## Notes

These are SVG workups rather than raster image-generation drafts so the title text remains exact and the cards can scale cleanly when reused.

All versions are black-and-white with transparent area outside the card shape.

The first-pass road sign direction is probably too prop-like for a universal title card.

The newspaper-print direction is better aligned with a reusable comic title bug, especially because it can evoke `The Around Here Chronicle` without requiring the full newspaper concept.

The best candidates from the second pass are likely:

- v5 newspaper masthead, if the title card can be moderately wide
- v8 compact newspaper corner bug, if the title card needs to stay small in the upper-left corner

Standing rule:

Use v18 as the approved `Around Here...` title-card asset for comic-page overlays.

It is a Sunday-comics feature title, not an in-world sign, newspaper clipping, label, or prop.

Default placement is the upper-left corner. The title card has page-title priority and may cover incidental border details, height marks, background texture, or nonessential art if needed.

New comic pages should include the title card during initial generation whenever practical. If not included at generation time, add it as a post-generation overlay before approval.
