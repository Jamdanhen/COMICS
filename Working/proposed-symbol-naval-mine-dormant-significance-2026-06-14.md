# Proposed Symbol - Naval Mine / Dormant Significance

Status: proposed symbol / pending Taskmaster approval

Source:

Taskmaster definition, 2026-06-14.

## Core Meaning

Naval Mine:

- Dormant significance.
- Not actively pursuing anything.
- Not hidden.
- Not moving.
- Not even necessarily dangerous.
- The danger comes from collision.
- Something eventually runs into it.
- Then the consequences occur.

## Working Interpretation

The naval mine is not the same as the `Mine` concept.

It is not a tunnel, structure, archive, habitat, or underground project space.

It represents a visible but inactive significance that can remain inert until contact happens. The event is not caused by pursuit or concealment; it is caused by eventual collision.

## Visual Guidance

Use when the scene needs:

- visible dormant significance
- an inert consequence waiting for contact
- a thing that does not chase, hide, or announce itself
- danger or consequence caused by collision rather than intention

Avoid using it when the intended meaning is:

- conceptual exploration
- project tunnels or layers
- containment
- deliberate sabotage
- an active trap
