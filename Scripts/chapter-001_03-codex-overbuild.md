## Series Function

This page introduces Codex through implementation behavior.

Codex receives the living idea as a practical problem, then builds an unnecessarily elaborate machine that gently segments it into usable parts. The joke is not violence or destruction; it is that a simple act of idea breakdown becomes an overbuilt machine with gauges, levers, pipes, bells, whistles, doodads, and extra systems.

## Six-Panel Beat

1. Taskmaster and Razorforge encounter the soft one-eyed idea creature emerging from a pipe or hatch.
2. Codex arrives with parts and tools.
3. Codex begins building a gentle processing machine around the idea.
4. The machine becomes wildly overbuilt with extra mechanisms.
5. The idea passes through a non-violent segmenting process.
6. The segmented idea emerges in a compartmentalized tray, with several separate pieces carrying their own small eyes.
