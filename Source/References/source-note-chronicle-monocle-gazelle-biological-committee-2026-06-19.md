# Source Note - Chronicle, Monocle, Gazelle, Biological Committee

Status: source reference / not canon by itself

Source:

Taskmaster clarification in chat on 2026-06-19 while developing individual symbol language for a future visual dictionary page.

## Around Here Chronicle

The Around Here Chronicle is a subset of Around Here.

It is the newspaper that reports for the undefined location where unusual things happen.

Around Here is the jurisdiction / undefined location.

The Chronicle is the reporting institution inside that jurisdiction.

## Monocle

The Monocle is connected to articles in the Around Here Chronicle.

It is like a zoomed-in article or focus article from the Chronicle.

It may be opinion, analysis, or another kind of focused treatment.

It is not simply generic interpretation. It is a Chronicle-style focused article/lens.

The joke origin is partly sound-based:

`Monocle` rhymes with `Chronicle`.

The Monocle focuses on one thing, so the joke is the Monocle and the Chronicle: the Chronicle reports broadly, while the Monocle zooms in.

## Gazelle

The Gazelle is an anthropomorphic gazelle reporter.

The Gazelle goes out and reports stories for the Around Here Chronicle.

The Gazelle is not merely a generic observation icon; it is a character/role figure tied to reporting.

Origin:

`Gazelle` came from the abandoned first name of the paper, the `Around Here Gazette`.

A misspelling or drift from `Gazette` to `Gazelle` became literalized as a gazelle reporting the news.

## Biological Committee

The Biological Committee is one of the many committees that rule the barriers to working on projects or creating ideas.

Its pressures include:

- sleep
- hunger
- time
- physical limits

The Biological Committee does not necessarily need a single mascot or creature symbol.

It issues violations and reminders.

## Use Boundary

This note preserves Taskmaster's clarification. It should guide symbol drafts and future dictionary work, but it does not by itself create final visual canon.
