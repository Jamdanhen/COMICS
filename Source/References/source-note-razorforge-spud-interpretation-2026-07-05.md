## Core Interpretation

Razorforge's revised reading is that the Spud should not be treated as a mascot.

The Spud is a living unresolved thing that was recognized, not invented.

Taskmaster does not create the Spud in the approved comic sequence. Taskmaster
notices it, sketches it, revisits it, and observes it crossing containers.

The key behavioral distinction:

```text
The Spud is not born.
The Spud is noticed.
```

## Container-Crossing Pattern

The approved pages show the Spud moving through containers:

- thought
- notebook
- repeated sketches
- computer screen
- interpreted anatomy
- blueprint
- machine/process space
- preservation shelf

This matters because the Spud is not merely growing larger. It is crossing
boundaries between media, tools, systems, and interpretations.

The current interpretive sequence is:

```text
Paper
Computer
Chat
Project files
Registry
```

The Spud keeps growing, and the surrounding ecosystem keeps adapting to it.

## Role Interpretation

Taskmaster observes and preserves.

Razorforge interprets, compresses, questions, maps, and builds understanding.

Codex implements around the living unresolved thing, often with excessive
machinery and incomplete stopping conditions.

The Chief Inspector classifies and tests categories.

The Registry gives unresolved or cross-project material a place to remain
findable without forcing premature authority.

## Philosophical Turn

The strongest reading from the approved packet is not:

```text
Finish everything.
Decide everything.
Classify everything.
```

It is:

```text
Preserve the interesting thing until reality tells you what it is.
```

This connects the Spud, the Noodle Bowl, and the Fragile Detail Jar.

## Splinter / Scratch Pattern

Razorforge connected the Noodle Bowl and unresolved-symbol behavior to two
related experiences:

- a cognitive anomaly: something does not fit, and the mind cannot ignore it
- a behavioral return: the unresolved thing keeps drawing attention back to
  itself

The Around Here addition is preservation.

Instead of forcing immediate resolution, the comic language gives unresolved
significance somewhere to sit:

- Noodle Bowl
- Fragile Detail Jar
- Crate / Unknown
- candidate symbol register
- Registry relationship or boundary note

The unresolved thing is not always solved immediately. Sometimes it is held
until its actual function becomes visible.

## Behavioral Vocabulary Reading

Razorforge's key conclusion is that the symbol key is not a simple glossary.

It is a behavioral vocabulary.

The objects are verbs disguised as nouns:

- Noodle Bowl: hold uncertainty
- Fragile Detail Jar: preserve
- Beam: support
- Mine: discover
- Roads / Connections: relate
- Rook / Taskmaster Tool: steward
- Razorforge Symbol: interpret and compress
- Codex Machine: implement, usually beyond moderation
- Tool Stamp: mark action or ownership when provenance matters

This is why the approved comics work as more than jokes or illustrations.
They teach the reader how Around Here behaves.
