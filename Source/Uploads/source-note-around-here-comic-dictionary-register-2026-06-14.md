## Taskmaster Prioritization Note

For actual comic development, the likely first dictionary page should contain:

- Around Here
- The Noodle
- The Rubber Chicken
- The Rook
- The Crate
- Zodak's Helmet

Rationale:

Those six currently carry the most conceptual weight without requiring too much explanation.

The rest feel more like residents of Around Here than foundational landmarks.
