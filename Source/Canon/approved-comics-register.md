## Approved Shelf Naming Convention

Use two reader-facing lanes:

- `dictionary-###-short-title.png` for front matter, visual keys, character introductions, and other pages that explain how to read the comic.
- `chapter-###-page-###-short-title.png` for narrative pages in reader-facing sequence.

Dictionary pages may be read before the numbered chapters, but they are not chapter pages.

Chapter placement is allowed to differ from creation order. Approved images can be saved while still being placed later in the intended reading sequence.
