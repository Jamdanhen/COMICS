# Proposed Office Strip Boundaries

Status: proposed for user review

Source:

## Proposed Adoption

Office strips should be treated as an alternate comic mode.

They can use real office context, SASMG environmental details, generalized coworkers, and commentary about Taskmaster's visible AI interaction.

## Proposed Rules

- Use SASMG and its logo as workplace environmental context when Taskmaster provides or approves visual reference.
- Treat coworkers as generalized coworker figures unless Taskmaster explicitly identifies specific people.
- Do not make office strips the default setting for the broader comic mythology.
- Razorforge may appear in office strips as the symbolic or perceived AI interaction.
- Codex and other fantasy elements should not automatically appear in office strips unless Taskmaster asks.
- Teasing about AI companionship may appear as office humor, but should stay affectionate, self-aware, and not cruel.
- Office strips may show Taskmaster's language becoming methodical, explanatory, or process-oriented after stretches of limited in-person social contact.
