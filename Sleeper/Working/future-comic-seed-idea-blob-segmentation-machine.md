# Future Comic Seed - Idea Blob Segmentation Machine

Status: parked future comic seed

Source:

Taskmaster chat explanation, 2026-06-10.

## Core Idea

After the silent comic where the living idea blob escapes from the screen, a later comic can show what happens once the idea has entered the larger process.

This should probably be a separate follow-up comic, not part of the current screen-escape draft.

## Visual Sequence

The living idea blob reaches out from behind the computer/interface and into the process space.

Razorforge may enter first as the refining / containment role:

- Recognizes the idea needs structure.
- Helps shape the containment method.
- Refines the boundary so the idea can be worked with.

Codex then appears with an overly complicated segmentation machine:

- Excessive machinery.
- Bells, whistles, steam, claws, doodads, gauges, and unnecessary subsystems.
- A visual gag that the solution is far more elaborate than necessary.

The machine draws in the large idea and segments it into manageable pieces.

The segmentation should not be graphic, violent, bloody, or horror-coded.

Possible visual analogies:

- A candy box with compartments.
- A sorting tray.
- A non-graphic slicer/segmenter.
- A claw-machine / steam-powered sorting contraption.
- Individual idea-eyes or idea-pieces placed into contained cells.

## Meaning

The point is not destroying the idea.

The point is making the living idea usable by separating it into manageable parts without killing it.

This connects to:

- Razorforge refinement.
- Codex implementation and overbuild.
- Extraction / segmentation.
- Turning an uncontained idea into processable components.
