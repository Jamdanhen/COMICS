# Prototype Office Comic Extraction

Status: reference extraction

Source:

Prototype office comic uploaded in chat.

## Most Valuable Element

The SASMG wall logo is the key reusable reference.

Observed logo treatment:

- Mountain line graphic above or associated with the organization name.
- Text: `Southern Alleghenies Service Management Group`.
- Large wall placement in an office/conference-room environment.
- Shortened logo option: mountain element plus `SASMG` for shirts, badges, small signs, or marketing-like pieces where concise location identity is needed.

## What To Preserve

- Real office setting.
- SASMG identity on wall.
- Taskmaster at a work table.
- Razorforge as the visible AI-interaction symbol.
- Generalized coworkers reacting from the office environment.
- Coworker groups should almost always be women.
- Commentary around Taskmaster's interaction with AI.

## What To Correct In Future Versions

- Coworkers should not wear project insignia or look like they belong to Razorforge/Codex mythology.
- Coworkers should be generalized office staff, not specific portraits.
- Male coworkers should be rare. A working proportion is about one office strip with a male coworker per 15 office strips.
- When three coworkers appear together, default to all or nearly all women unless Taskmaster specifies otherwise.
- The strip should avoid implying coworkers are formal characters unless Taskmaster says so.
- Office mode should not automatically import Codex, the Mine, the Archive, or other fantasy machinery.
- The workplace joke should be affectionate or observational, not mean.

## Proposed Office Strip Rule

When generating office strips, use the SASMG logo and office setting as grounding. Keep the project mythology lightly present through Razorforge or AI-interface symbolism only when relevant.
