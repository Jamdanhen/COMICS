## Concept

Information begins as a tiny living form and grows through successive capture systems.

The joke is not that old tools are bad and new tools are good.

The joke is that living information keeps outgrowing the container until Taskmaster builds a better system.

1. Tiny idea creature on a primitive tablet.
2. Notebook capture.
3. Dictation / recording.
4. Transcript and session-log structure.
5. Extraction / source-tag handling.
6. Human-facing control / stable containment.
