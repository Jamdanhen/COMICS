# Executive Director Role Grounding

Status: approved source grounding

Purpose:

Preserve how Taskmaster's Executive Director role should influence the comic project without making county human-services administration a frequent literal subject.

## Approved Framing

The Executive Director role is useful source material, but it belongs in a different category than the comic's main mythology.

It should be treated mainly as grounding reference for Taskmaster.

## Taskmaster Influence

Taskmaster carries:

- Stewardship
- Responsibility
- Prioritization
- Continuity
- Final authority

Taskmaster understands systems with real human consequences.

Taskmaster is used to balancing:

- Funding
- Oversight
- Provider agencies
- Rules
- Competing needs
- Practical outcomes

The comic can borrow the shape of that role without turning the comic into county human-services administration.

## Character Posture

This role should influence Taskmaster's posture as:

- Accountable
- System-aware
- Protective of process
- Aware that decisions affect real people
- Allergic to useless complexity when it increases burden

## Use Boundary

Do not make the Executive Director role a recurring plot focus unless Taskmaster explicitly asks.

Use it as background gravity for Taskmaster's authority, judgment, and sensitivity to burdensome systems.
